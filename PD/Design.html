Poster

In this poster, I try to focus on myself, what I like and put it all together, I am a PC enthusiast, I love to build desktops, I do a lot of troubleshooting of PC too, I love to video edit as well, especially making montages out of my own recorded gameplays and always have a mindset that I should think outside the box when needed or stuck at any problems.

So starting off the design, I just used my logo as the whole theme, basically a huge version of my logo but also represent what I do, what I love doing and basically me. There is no words or fonts used as everything is created manually, I am trying to show through visual how and who I am as a person.

Namecard

So all these namecards, I have chosen to make the whole design simple but also profession at the same time. I have chose to use the font, “Century Gothic” as it is a modernize font influence by geometric style sans serif which was in advertisments.

The colour scheme I went for is Complementary colours as it is a simple colour which is vibrant and also can help one colour stand out more, so in this case, I used blue and yellow, I try to make my name stand out so I used yellow to be the “main” colour.

Typography

The main objective of this design is to identify myself using typography. So the first thing I would do is to list down the things that I find I like or more closer relevant to me such as who I was, what am I like, my personality .

I made used of my name to make up the shape of a box and a lightbulb. Although it is a simple box, I have used to form, shape, proportion and scale to make it look 3D
